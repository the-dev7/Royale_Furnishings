THE STORY




Röyale Furnishings was established back in 1982 where three excellent furnituremakers once saw the dream to raise some money to make their own brand of furnitures. We had our first store in a rented storeroom in Old St, Plaistow, London.

Today, due to efforts put forward these people we stand at the top in the market and are the most prestigeous brands to make premium furnitures for you.
